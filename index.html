Services

  IT Consultancy

Product Based Consultancy: We provide complete consultancy on product design, architecture, technology, programming, testing and release of the product. Our expert's team of consultants provides complete support and maintenance to upgrade and further release of the product time to time.


Project Based Consultancy: We cater to a diversified set of businesses for project consultancy services. We offer our consultancy services at any stage of the project and help the client to bring efficient and cost effective IT solutions to leverage the growth of their organization.

  Software Development

Custom Application Development: When packages software solutions aren't sufficient and flexible enough for your organization, our custom application development services is the best suited solutions for your short term or long term project requirements.


Offshore Application Development: Application development outsourcing helps clients to workout latest technology upgrades with ever evolving business processes and operations. Our mature offshore delivery model helps organizations achieve cost effectiveness, scalability, quality and on time delivery.

  Enterprise Solutions

ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning): We provide custom ERP development and implementation of various industry standard ERP packages. Our team provides complete maintenance, timely updates and after sales support services as per the client needs.


Application Re-engineering: We offer complete feasibility study and migration solution for existing legacy application. Our consultants in application re-engineering focuses on designing such an architecture which reduces the maintenance cost, leverages the performance and extend flexibility in the application.

  Staff Augmentation Services
